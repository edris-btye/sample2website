What is Fashion? Fashion is the art, industry, and practice of creating, promoting, and wearing clothing, accessories, and lifestyle products that reflect cultural trends, personal identity, and social status. It is a form of self-expression and a reflection of the times. Key Aspects of Fashion Clothing & Apparel: Includes everyday wear, formal wear, ethnic wear, and haute couture. Accessories: Bags, shoes, hats, jewelry, watches, and eyewear. Trends & Styles: Fashion trends change seasonally and are influenced by designers, celebrities, social media, and cultural events. Fashion Industry: Comprises designers, brands, retailers, magazines, influencers, and online platforms. Sustainability: Increasingly, fashion emphasizes eco-friendly materials, ethical production, and reducing waste
